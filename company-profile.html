Who We Are

Radhe Industries is a leading manufacturer of high-quality disposable cups, established in 2012. We provide cafes, restaurants, hotels, and businesses with premium disposable drinkware — crafted for convenience, safety, and hygiene. We believe that serving beverages should be effortless, without compromising on quality or cleanliness.
