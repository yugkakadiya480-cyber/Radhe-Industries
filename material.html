Premium Materials

We use only the finest food-grade materials to ensure safety, durability, and sustainability.

Polypropylene (PP)

Polypropylene is our primary material for hot and cold beverage cups. It is known for its excellent heat resistance, clarity, and recyclability.

  • Heat Resistant up to 120°C
  • 100% Recyclable
  • BPA-Free & Food Safe

PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate)

PET is used for our premium transparent cups. It offers crystal-clear visibility, making it perfect for cold coffees, juices, and desserts.

  • Superior Clarity
  • High Impact Resistance
  • Widely Recyclable

Our Commitment to Safety

All materials used at Radhe Industries are sourced from certified global suppliers. We ensure every batch is tested for food safety compliance and environmental impact.
