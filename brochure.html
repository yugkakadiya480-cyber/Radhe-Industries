About Radhe Industries

Crafting Excellence Since 2012

Radhe Industries is a leading manufacturer of high-quality disposable cups based in Rajkot, Gujarat. With two state-of-the-art production units and a passionate team, we deliver products that meet the highest standards of hygiene, safety, and durability.

Our products are manufactured using premium food-grade polypropylene (PP) and PET materials, rigorously tested at every stage to ensure zero compromise on quality.
